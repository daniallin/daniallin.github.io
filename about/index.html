About Me

I received my PhD from Department of Mechanics and Engineering Science, Peking University (2022.6)

My academic research interests: microstructure mechanics, strain gradient viscoelasticity, biomechanics, finite element method, Artifical Inteligence application in mechanics.

If you are interested in my research, do not hesitate to contact me. My e-mail, linzy AT pku.edu.cn

Also, I am interested in artificial intelligence (AI). I did some works during 2017 - 2020. My GitHub: https://github.com/daniallin, maybe you can find something useful. Now, I am doing something about the application of graph neural network in finite element and other simulation methods.
